Site Plan - Online Product Store

Site Name

Ayisek Store

This name was selected because it directly represents the purpose of the website: to serve as an online platform for showcasing and selling a variety of products.

Site Purpose

The site provides a comprehensive e-commerce platform for various products. It offers an easy-to-navigate interface for customers to browse, view details, and purchase products online.

Scenarios

  • How can I find the latest electronics available on the store?
  • Where can I read reviews for a specific product?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #4CAF50 (used for headings, buttons, and accents)

Secondary Color: #FF9800 (used for links, highlights, and secondary buttons)

Typography

Primary Font: Roboto, sans-serif (used for body text and general content)

Secondary Font: Georgia, serif (used for headings and emphasis text)
